Founder & Lead Practitioner

Hoon Lee

L.Ac. · DAOM(c) · CALee Acupuncture, Buena Park CA

Hoon Lee is a licensed acupuncturist and the founder of CALee Acupuncture. He studied Statistics at Korea University, shaping his view of the body as an interconnected system. His work defines Korean acupuncture as a system of diagnostic reasoning — an interpretive, patient-centered process grounded in relational imbalances and constitutional patterns. His research integrates classical principles with analytical thinking to clarify acupuncture as a coherent model of individualized care.
