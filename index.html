Korean Acupuncture Is Not Generic Acupuncture

Most acupuncture clinics use standardized point protocols for each diagnosis. At CALee, treatment begins with constitutional diagnosis — identifying the underlying pattern driving your symptoms.

Two patients with the same diagnosis receive different treatments. Point selection, technique, and session structure all follow your constitutional type.
