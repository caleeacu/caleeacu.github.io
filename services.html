Why Korean Acupuncture Is Different

Standard acupuncture protocols treat the symptom. Korean acupuncture — rooted in Sasang and Eight Constitution Medicine — treats the constitutional pattern behind the symptom.

Two patients with identical diagnoses receive different treatments. Point selection, technique, and session structure all follow your constitutional type.
